Kare-Kare

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg oxtail, cut into serving pieces
  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup annatto powder
  • 1 bundle string beans
  • 1 banana blossom, sliced
  • 1 eggplant, sliced
  • 2 cups water
  • Bagoong (fermented shrimp paste) for serving

Steps:

  1. Boil oxtail until tender, then set aside.
  2. In a pot, heat annatto powder in oil and add peanut butter.
  3. Add water and stir to create a sauce.
  4. Add boiled oxtail and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Add vegetables and cook until tender.
  6. Serve with bagoong and steamed rice.
